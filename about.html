About the Hub

A welcoming place for support, learning, and connection

The Community Wellbeing Hub brings together health advice, practical services, events, and friendly local partnerships so residents can find help in one trusted space.

Our Mission

Helping local people feel healthier, stronger, and more connected

We focus on prevention, inclusion, and community confidence. That means making support easy to access, designing events that feel genuinely welcoming, and creating services that respond to what residents actually need.

How We Work

One hub, many ways to take part

Drop in for advice. Speak to our team about local services, wellbeing support, and next steps.
Join a regular activity. Choose from social groups, exercise sessions, workshops, or family events.
Build confidence over time. We help residents keep momentum with practical follow-up and referrals.
What Makes Us Different

Practical support that feels personal

Residents often need more than one service at once. We make that easier by bringing wellbeing, advice, events, and local signposting together in one place.

That means fewer barriers, clearer next steps, and a space that feels consistent whether someone is visiting for the first time or returning for ongoing support.
